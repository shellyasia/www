## What is Shelly RGBW2?

### WiFi-operated RGBW Controller

Transform your LED strips into smart lighting with one simple step. The Shelly RGBW2 connects like a standard LED controller and lets you control all your lights directly from your smartphone, tablet, or voice assistant.

### Features

- No hub required
- Supports wide voltage range (12V/24V DC)
- Extensive color palette, dimming options, and dynamic effects
- Compact design fits most standard electrical boxes and switches
- Fully compatible with other Shelly devices
- Works with Android, iOS, Amazon Alexa, Google Assistant, and home automation platforms via MQTT, CoAP, and REST API
- Ideal for integrating with Arduino projects in smart home automation

### Power Specifications

| Parameter | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Power Supply (AC) | Not supported |
| Power Supply (DC) | 12V / 24V |
| DC Output (12V per channel) | 45W |
| DC Output (24V per channel) | 90W |
| Combined DC Output (12V) | 144W |
| Combined DC Output (24V) | 288W |

## Use Cases

### Universal Lighting Control

Need to turn off all the lights before going to bed? With Shelly RGBW2, you can control every light in your home with just one tap on your smartphone—or even by voice command. It supports RGB+W lighting, four individual single-color LED strips, or spotlights in your living room.
